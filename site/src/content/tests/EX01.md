Expands a SNOMED is-a hierarchy slice with pagination. Pool covers 100 SNOMED hierarchies across three tiers — 60 large (≥5,000 descendants), 20 mid (500–4,999), 20 small (20–499) — with count/offset combinations sized to each tier.
