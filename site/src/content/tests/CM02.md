Translates codes using ConceptMaps bundled in FHIR R4 core and IPS packages. Covers FHIR-to-v2/v3 crosswalks (`cm-administrative-gender-v3`), status maps (`sc-*`), LOINC↔SNOMED mappings, severity and absence concept maps.
